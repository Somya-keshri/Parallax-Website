Grasses & Trees

In nature, I enjoy trees and streams and the smell of fresh trees and grass more than anything. The special silence of nature and the sound of the wind that wraps around the branches and the sound of birds are pleasing to me. I take refuge in nature to escape the noise of the city and the cars and the noise of people.Imagine a world without plants and trees! (scary, right!?) I believe that plants and trees make the earth so beautiful. They are also very healing. Human beings as well as animals were created to live in close harmony with nature.

The beauty of nature is unimaginable. Everyone is attracted by nature’s beauty and diversity. There are several natural attractions in different parts of the world.
